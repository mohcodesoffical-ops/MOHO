MOH Features

Explore all the ways Machan Oru Help makes student life smarter, easier, and more fun!

Doubt Buster
Got a question? Get an answer—instantly!
Stuck on a tricky math problem or a confusing concept? Just ask MOH and get clear, step-by-step solutions in seconds. No more waiting for replies in group chats—your personal study buddy is always on standby!
Study Scheduler
Your perfect timetable, made in seconds.
Say goodbye to messy planners and last-minute cramming. MOH creates a personalized study timetable based on your syllabus, deadlines, and free time. Stay organized, stress less, and ace those exams with a plan that actually fits your life.
Scholarship Scout
Never miss a local scholarship again!
MOH keeps an eye out for scholarships and grants in your area, sending you timely alerts tailored to your profile. Apply with confidence and boost your chances of funding your dreams—no more FOMO on free money!
Career Compass
Find your path, step by step.
Not sure what’s next after school or college? MOH maps out personalized career roadmaps, showing you courses, skills, and milestones for your dream job. Navigate your future with clarity and confidence.
Mind Check
Because your mental health matters.
MOH checks in with you regularly, offering quick mood check-ins and resources for stress, anxiety, or just a rough day. Get tips, support, and a friendly nudge to take care of yourself—because you’re more than just your grades.
Mojo Boost
Motivation, delivered daily.
Need a pep talk before that big test? MOH sends you motivational quotes, reminders, and positive vibes to keep your spirits high. Stay inspired and keep moving forward—even on Mondays!
Event Explorer
Discover what’s happening around you.
From workshops to hackathons, MOH finds local events and learning opportunities tailored to your interests. Meet new people, learn new skills, and never miss out on what’s happening in your student community.
Quiz Master
Prep smarter, not harder.
Practice makes perfect! MOH offers quick, interactive quizzes based on your syllabus to help you prep for exams. Track your progress, spot weak areas, and turn revision into a game.
Language Buddy
Translate, understand, communicate—anytime.
MOH helps you break language barriers with instant translation and explanation of tricky terms. Whether it’s homework or chatting with friends, you’ll never get lost in translation again.
Opportunity Radar
Be the first to know about internships and jobs.
MOH scans the web for internships and job openings that match your skills and interests. Get alerts, tips, and application deadlines—land your dream role before anyone else!
News Flash
Stay updated, stay ahead.
MOH delivers daily news, campus updates, and trending topics straight to your dashboard. Stay informed without doomscrolling—just the stuff that matters to students.
Chill Zone
Study hard, play harder!
All work and no play? Not here! MOH serves up fun facts, jokes, and quick games to give your brain a break. Recharge, laugh, and return to your books refreshed.
Study Circle
Find your study squad, anytime.
MOH connects you with peers for group studies, discussions, and project collaborations. Share notes, solve doubts together, and make learning a team sport.
Money Minder
Budgeting made easy (and painless).
Track your expenses, set savings goals, and get smart tips for student life on a budget. MOH helps you manage your money so you can focus on what matters—like pizza and textbooks.
Project Genie
Stuck on a project? MOH’s got ideas!
Get AI-powered project suggestions, research guidance, and step-by-step help for your assignments. Impress your teachers and classmates with creative, well-executed projects—without the stress.
